What is Serverless ?

    Common Question:
    Are there no more servers ?

    Answer:
        No, there are still servers involved. “Serverless” is just a name. We could have called it “Jeff”.
        Serverless brings a shift in responsibility for developers.
        To no longer be involved in the management and configuration of a server.
        Doing so allows for more time to be invested in implementing any addition to the application.
